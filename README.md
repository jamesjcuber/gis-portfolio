# GIS Portfolio - James Cuber

## About Me
I studied Geographic Information Systems at the United States Military Academy at West Point (2023), where I built a strong foundation in spatial analysis, geospatial data processing, and mapping.

My work focuses on applying GIS to real-world problems through data-driven analysis and visualization. I have experience building spatial workflows, working with GeoJSON and routing data, and developing mapping solutions for applications.

I am currently expanding my portfolio with projects in infrastructure, urban systems, and spatial decision-making.
